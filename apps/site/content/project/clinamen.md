On July 2025 I attended to an exposition in Paris after seeing some ads on instagram of this massive instalation at the "Bourse de Paris":

![picture](/projects/clinamen/picture.png){height="300px"}

The inpiration for this project is the fact the Clinamen exposition is "adapted" to the venue it is being presented.

It started on the Artist's house where he used air-filled pools.

This is a hommage to create a **"web version"** of the exposition.
