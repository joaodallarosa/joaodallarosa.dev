Most write-ups get published only once a project is finished and tidy, which quietly erases the part that's most useful to someone else facing the same problem: the messy middle, where a decision could have gone either way and didn't yet have hindsight backing it up.

This site is set up so posts and project pages can go out in draft first — reviewed live, in context, before they're marked published — rather than sitting in a private notes app until they feel "done."

It also forces a bit of discipline on the site itself: if the publishing flow is annoying, that friction shows up immediately instead of staying theoretical.
